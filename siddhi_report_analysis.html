Recommended Tech & Feature Upgrades 🚀
1
📱 Responsive Field-Sales App Mode
Why: Sales reps are on the road. The current wide tables are built for desktop monitors.
Fix: Implement mobile media queries. Add collapsible sidebars, horizontal scrolling for tables, and card-based mobile views so reps can check stock instantly on their phones.
2
🔔 Automated "Smart" Alerts
Why: You shouldn't have to manually check the dashboard to find emergencies.
Fix: Generate a push or dashboard alert when: (a) A FAST RUNNER hits Minimum stock, (b) An SO is overdue >7 days, (c) A top customer hasn't ordered in 60 days.
3
📤 One-Click PDF Quotation Generator
Why: Repetitive manual quote creation leads to errors and delivery delays.
Fix: Connect Customer Master + Material Pricing → Auto-generate professional GST quotations in PDF with standard payment terms directly from the UI.
4
🤖 AI-Driven Sales Projection
Why: We currently rely heavily on static 3M / 12M averages.
Fix: Feed sales history into a forecasting model to parse trends across seasons and automatically flag items predicted to run out before they hit Minimum levels.
5
📦 Advanced PO Supplier Tracking
Why: We track Pending PO quantity, but evaluating supplier reliability is hard.
Fix: Add an "Expected Delivery Date" column. Build a Supplier Reliability Dashboard showing which suppliers (e.g., LAPP vs Eaton) are consistently late vs on-time.
6
🏷️ "At-Risk" Customer Health Score
Why: It's cheaper to retain a customer than find a new one.
Fix: Auto-compute a 1-100 score based on Reorder Frequency, Order Size, & Recency. Display falling scores in red so sales reps know who to call today to prevent churn.

06 · Inventory Management
Supply Chain Math & Replenishment
📘 Minimum (Safety)
1 Month Avg
Formula: Avg1Y × 1.0
DANGER ZONE. If stock drops here, place Emergency PO. Production stops if this clears.
📙 Reorder Trigger
1.5 Month Avg
Formula: Avg1Y × 1.5
ACTION ZONE. Routine PO trigger point allowing for standard 2-week supplier lead times.
📕 Maximum Limit
3 Month Avg
Formula: Avg1Y × 3.0
CAPITAL CEILING. Do not hold more than this. Anything > Max is 'Excess Stock' wasting cash.
Movement Class & Stocking Strategy Integration
ClassRulesOur StrategyPO Action Rule
FAST RUNNER 9+ active months/year General Stock Keep strictly between Reorder & Max. Bulk buy allowed.
SLOW RUNNER 3-8 active months/year Against Order Only keep Minimum. Order only when SO arrives.
NON-MOVING <3 active months/year Made to Order Do not stock. Drop-ship or back-to-back order only.
07 · Demand Forecasting
Trend Analysis & Procurement Planning
📈 Short-Term Demand: 3M Avg
Reacts fast to recent order spikes, big project wins, or seasonal pushes. Use this to determine if current month buys need to be inflated above normal.
📊 Baseline Demand: 1Y Avg
Smooths out the noise. This is the bedrock metric for establishing yearly supplier commitments and calculating Min/Max thresholds safely.
Trend Overlay: Understanding Growth
When 3M Avg > 1Y Avg:
Momentum is positive. You are selling faster now than the yearly norm. If this is a FAST RUNNER, you must pre-emptively increase your PO sizes; otherwise, you will stockout before the Reorder system catches up.

When 3M Avg < 1Y Avg:
Demand is softening. Do not blindly place POs based on Max stock limits. Defer standard Reorders until the short-term 3M line stabilizes.
08 · Customer Intelligence
Customer Segmentation & Retention Tactics
🔁 Core Repeats
Reward loyalty. Implement volume rebates. Institute quarterly "business review" calls with top 20 accounts. Defend against competitors.
🆕 Initial Wins
Zero defect tolerance on first 3 orders. Prove reliability. Expand wallet share by cross-selling (e.g., if they buy cables, pitch switchgears).
♻️ Rebuild/Resurrected
Discover why they left us last year. Fix the root cause (price? delivery?). Treat them delicately to ensure they stick around this time.
❌ Lost Accounts
Action within 45 days. Dispatch senior sales strictly for feedback gathering. Find out who beat us and adjust pricing catalog accordingly.
Risk Mitigation & Concentration Limits
  • Red Flag: Top 3 customers making up >50% revenue.
  • Red Flag: Any single project account crossing 25%.
  • Strategy: Aggressively build mid-tier "general stock" buyer base so major account fluctuations don't shake the total P&L.
Growing "Online Business" Channel
  • Online buyers are price-sensitive and demand speed.
  • SLA Target: Acknowledge/Quote all online queries < 2 Hours.
  • Track Quote-to-Order Conversion Rate specifically for this segment.
09 · Urgent Supply Chain Actions
Dashboard "To-Do" List Implementation
1. Expedite Now
Open "SC Planning" View.
Filter columns for Expedite > 0.

These are items where SO delivery is due BUT stock is low and you have a Pending PO. Call supplier to rush delivery today.
2. Execute Fresh POs
Filter for PO Need > 0.
Sort by Value Descending.

The top 20 items on this list are your immediate priority buys for the week to prevent upcoming stockouts on core items.
3. Flush Excess
Filter Excess Stock.

If these are NON-MOVING or SLOW-RUNNER, you have dead capital. Liquidate at cost price to free up warehouse space and cash for fast runners.
