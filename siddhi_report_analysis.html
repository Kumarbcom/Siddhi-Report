06 · Inventory Management
Stock Planning Framework
📗 Min Level
= Avg1Y × 1
Safety buffer. If stock falls below this → place PO immediately.
📙 Reorder Level
= Avg1Y × 1.5
Trigger PO at this level to account for lead time from supplier.
📕 Max Level
= Avg1Y × 3
Do not exceed this. Excess stock beyond Max = capital locked unnecessarily.
Stock Strategy Decision Matrix

Expedite  SO due & stock low → Pull in existing PO
07 · Demand Forecasting
Sales Projection & Planning
📈 3-Month Rolling Average (3M Avg)
Best for short-term PO planning. Captures seasonal demand, recent project wins, or new customer additions. If 3M > 1Y avg → demand is accelerating → stock up.
📊 12-Month Average (1Y Avg)
Best for long-term Min/Max levels. Smooths out seasonal spikes. Use for setting safety stock and annual PO planning with suppliers.
📉 YoY Trend Signal
3M > 1Y: Growing demand → increase Max level by 20%
3M < 1Y: Declining demand → reduce Max level by 20%
Equal: Stable – maintain current levels

08 · Customer Intelligence
Customer Retention & Growth Strategy
🔁 Repeat Customers
Offer volume discounts, priority delivery, and quarterly review meetings. Never let a repeat customer feel ignored.
🆕 New Customers
Assign 90-day nurture plan. First 3 orders should be flawless. Offer credit terms only after 2nd payment.
♻️ Rebuild Customers
They left but came back. Find out WHY they left and fix the root cause. These are high-risk high-reward accounts.
❌ Lost Customers
Contact within 30 days of identifying as "Lost". Lost to competition = pricing issue. Lost to silence = relationship issue.
Customer Concentration Risk
  • If top 3 customers >50% of revenue → HIGH RISK. Diversify immediately.
  • If any 1 customer >25% of revenue → Single-point-of-failure. Develop alternatives.
  • Target: No single group >20% of total revenue
Online Business – Growth Opportunity
  • Online Business channel is a new segment – track separately
  • Set up competitive pricing vs Amazon / IndiaMART
  • Fast response time SLA: Quote within 4 hours for online leads
  • Track conversion: Inquiry → Quote → Order rate monthly
